Marathon Work Out Plan

6 Week Integrated Program

By Noah Gonzalez

This work-out plan is designed for those who already run half-marathon distances

Week 1

Day 1: Lower Body

Targeted Muscles (main muscles): Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Adductors, Shins, and Calves.

Description:

Stretch and Warm Up will be performed under <VT1.

Blocks 1 - 2 will be performed between VT1 - VT2.

Instructions: Supersets! 1 Strenth exercise followed by 1 Cardio exercise. Target a different primary muscle for each exercise. Follow with a cardio move that targets the same muscle.

Stretch / Warm - Up: 10 - 15 minutes

Block 3

TEMPO RUN (30 Minutes)

Target your average pace within first 1/2 mile. Gradually increase pace by 10% by mile 1. Return to your average pace and increase at a 1:3 ratio (1 minute fast followed by 3 minutes of average pace) for 10 minutes.

After 10 minutes increase speed by 5% and increase average pace by 5% at 1:5 ratio for 5 minutes. Change to 1:3 ration for following 5 minutes.

Day 2: Push + Pull

Targeted Muscles (main muscles):
Push: Chest, Shoulders and Triceps Pull: Back, Biceps, Forearms, Qadriceps, Hamstrings, Abdominals, and Calves.

Description:

Stretch and Warm Up will be performed under <VT1.

All sets will be performed between VT1 - VT2. Last 5 reps in Set 2 should be VT2>

Instructions: 6 exercises Performed in 3 set. Finish with a burn out.

Stretch / Warm - Up: 10 - 15 minutes

Block 2

Burn Out - 5 Minutes
